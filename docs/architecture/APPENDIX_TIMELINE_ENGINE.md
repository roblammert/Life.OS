# APPENDIX_TIMELINE_ENGINE.md  
**Timeline Engine — Life.OS**  
*Part of the Life.OS Architecture Documentation Suite*

The Timeline Engine organizes all entries, tasks, habits, insights, Life Moments, reviews, and context states into a unified chronological narrative. It provides the backbone for temporal navigation, life chapters, arcs, and long‑term pattern visualization across Life.OS.

## Purpose

The Timeline Engine gives Life.OS a coherent sense of time. It anchors every object—entries, tasks, habits, insights, concepts, memories—into a structured timeline that supports reflection, pattern detection, narrative understanding, and long‑term personal growth.

---

## Timeline Architecture

The engine uses a layered architecture:

- **Event layer** — raw chronological events  
- **Aggregation layer** — groups events into days, weeks, months, years  
- **Narrative layer** — identifies arcs, chapters, and transitions  
- **Context layer** — overlays emotional, cognitive, and productivity states  
- **Visualization layer** — renders the timeline in UI  
- **Integration layer** — connects to reviews, Life Moments, and memory  

This architecture supports both granular and high‑level temporal understanding.

---

## Timeline Data Model

The engine stores:

- **timeline_event_id**  
- **event_type** — entry, task, habit, insight, moment, review, context shift  
- **timestamp**  
- **temporal_bucket** — day, week, month, year  
- **context_snapshot** — emotional, cognitive, productivity metadata  
- **linked_objects** — references to related entries, tasks, insights, etc.  
- **arc_metadata** — arc position, slope, intensity  
- **chapter_metadata** — chapter boundaries and transitions  

Timeline events are immutable and versioned.

---

## Timeline Event Types

The engine organizes:

- **Entries** — journal entries, notes  
- **Tasks** — completions, postponements  
- **Habits** — streak updates, consistency changes  
- **Insights** — emotional, cognitive, habit, productivity, conceptual  
- **Life Moments** — breakthroughs, shifts, milestones  
- **Reviews** — daily, weekly, monthly, yearly  
- **Context states** — emotional, cognitive, productivity, environmental  
- **Graph updates** — concept emergence, theme shifts  
- **Memory updates** — long‑term pattern reinforcement  

Each event contributes to the user’s evolving narrative.

---

## Temporal Aggregation

The engine groups events into:

- **Daily views** — entries, tasks, habits, insights, context  
- **Weekly views** — patterns, cycles, productivity arcs  
- **Monthly views** — conceptual evolution, habit consistency  
- **Yearly views** — identity themes, life chapters, long‑term arcs  

Aggregation supports both micro and macro reflection.

---

## Life Chapters

The engine identifies life chapters based on:

- Conceptual themes  
- Emotional arcs  
- Cognitive cycles  
- Habit and productivity patterns  
- Major Life Moments  
- Context shifts  
- Long‑term memory evolution  

Chapters appear in yearly reviews and the timeline UI.

---

## Arcs and Patterns

The engine tracks:

- **Emotional arcs** — rising, falling, stabilizing  
- **Cognitive arcs** — clarity cycles, overload periods  
- **Behavioral arcs** — habit formation, collapse, recovery  
- **Productivity arcs** — focus cycles, bottlenecks  
- **Conceptual arcs** — theme evolution  
- **Identity arcs** — long‑term self‑development  

Arcs are visualized as curves or segments on the timeline.
